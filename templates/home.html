About Us

What is the CodeTronics club ?

We are bringing CodeTronics, much more than a club, where we all together will learn and play with the codes and the electronics with the core thought of innovating ideas. Let's join the different face of Code & Electronics. Let's join CodeTronics.

Revolution is happening in front of your eyes Coding and circuit designing is the base of every smart technology you see in front of you. With "Code Tronics " - an initiative taken by the ECE department of NIET, we wish to make you the part of that same revolution. Our mission is to develop the interest of coding and circuit designing in every possible student within our reach so that YOU can cultivate the ideas and thrive off that creativity and impulse .So be a part of this revolution or be a spectator forever.

What do We do?

We aim to organise two events in a month. One for coding other for electronics.Events include various Competitions,seminars,Quizzes,talks.

VISION

Electronics is no more just a component in modern technology; it is the basis of assessment of a country’s progress. It is one of the most knowledge-intensive and dynamic fields of study. It is the field that is most crucial to tackling national problems of illiteracy, healthcare and safety which imparts it with tremendous potential in India.

With the knowledge of this fact,CodeTronics, NIET, endeavors to create a community of students driven by a passion for electronics and innovation by acting as a means for them to learn and challenge what they have learnt. We aim to turn students into visionaries who can create a better society with their love for technology.

MISSION

Our mission is to enhance the coding and circuit designing skills and develop the interest among the students for the coming future technology in the field of IT and Electronics
